Swiss TableTop

How to use this app:

  1. Enter the players present for your game day.
  2. Generate your first "random" round of games.
  3. Play your games and then input the scores, the app will then generate your next round of games using the Swiss-pairings system!

We recommend having at least 6 to 8 players to get the most out of the Swiss system.
